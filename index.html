The Bloop Troop

A Short Film by Pencillati Studios

A ruthless barbarian, a hair-splitting master archer and a knight in shining armour are brought together in the battlefield by a common enemy- a formidable fly. What ensues in this battle of epic proportions tests everyone’s skills and takes an age-old war that every human has wanted to win, to a whole new level.
